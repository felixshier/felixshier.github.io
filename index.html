Alpha-Light

Merlin Neurotechnology

This project was about the configuration of elements in a smart home environment, specifically a Phillips Hue smart light, using the Internet of Things (IoT) and a Muse headset to read and collect Electroencephalography (EEG) signals. With everything put together, we were able to wirelessly control the color spectrum of the Phillips Hue smart light based on a user's brain state, which was determined by analyzing the EEG signals measured by the Muse headset.

Meeting Minutes

Betalab

This project was about leveraging Natural Language Processing (NLP) capabilities to develop a service capable of outputting summarized meeting minutes in the form of text. The model that I developed was an unsupervised extractive model. This involved preprocessing the text data to ensure it was in its most effective form for the Deep Averaging Network (DAN) encoder that was used to determine the encodings of the sentences. Once the sentences were encodded, they were grouped using meanshift clustering such that the cardinality of the set of sentences could be reduced.
